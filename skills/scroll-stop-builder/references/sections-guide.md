# Sections Guide

Use this structure for premium scroll-stop landing pages.

## Recommended Page Flow

### 1. Hero Lockup
- Full-bleed hero or framed stage
- One clear headline
- One concise subhead
- Motion starts immediately but gently

### 2. Sticky Sequence
- Keep the visual stage pinned
- Advance copy in 3-6 beats
- Each beat should explain one shift in the video or value prop

### 3. Proof / Payload Strip
- Surface the interesting objects, components, or benefits
- Use compact cards or captions
- Keep it scannable

### 4. Offer or Service Explanation
- Translate the spectacle into something useful
- Who it is for
- What they get
- Why it matters

### 5. CTA Finish
- End on a calm, high-contrast call to action
- Give the eye somewhere to rest after the visual intensity

## Motion Patterns

- `sticky-stage + text swap`: safest default
- `frame sequence scrub`: best for controlled product motion
- `layered parallax`: good when the video itself stays as a loop
- `chapter reveal`: useful when the source video has 3-5 strong beats

## Mobile Rules

- Reduce heavy parallax
- Replace frame scrubbing with poster frames or short loops when needed
- Keep copy outside dense video regions
- Prioritize readability over spectacle

## Performance Rules

- Compress images or frames aggressively
- Lazy-load non-hero assets
- Use fewer frames on mobile
- Prefer a small number of strong moments over dozens of mediocre steps
